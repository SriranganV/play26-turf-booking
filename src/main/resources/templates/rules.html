House Rules & Guidelines

To ensure a safe, fair, and highly enjoyable experience for everyone, all players and visitors must adhere to the following TurfPlay facility and sports guidelines.

🏟 Facility Guidelines

📅

Bookings & Timing

All bookings must be made in advance. Please arrive 10 minutes prior to your scheduled slot. Your session strictly ends at the designated time to respect the next booking.

👥

On-Pitch Conduct

Aggressive behavior and abusive language will result in an immediate ban. Spitting, food, and chewing gum are strictly not allowed inside the playing area.

👟

Gear & Equipment

Only flat-soled turf shoes or trainers are permitted. Metal studs are prohibited. TurfPlay equipment (bibs, balls) must be returned after the game.
