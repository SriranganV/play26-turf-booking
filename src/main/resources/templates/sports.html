Football / Futsal

The world's game. Whether you are playing 5-a-side on a boxed turf or an 11-a-side full pitch match, football offers unmatched high-intensity interval training. It demands quick reflexes, intense stamina, and perfect team coordination.

  • Burns up to 600 calories per hour
  • Improves lower-body agility and footwork
  • Builds elite cardiovascular endurance

Box Cricket

A fast-paced, high-energy adaptation of traditional cricket played in enclosed turfs. It focuses on aggressive batting, tight bowling, and lightning-fast fielding. Because of the enclosed space, the ball is always in play, making it a thrilling experience.

  • Enhances hand-eye coordination and timing
  • Great for explosive sprints and core rotation
  • Highly strategic team-building environment

Tennis

A phenomenal full-body workout that challenges your tactical thinking just as much as your physical limits. Whether playing singles or doubles, tennis requires rapid lateral movements, powerful strikes, and unwavering focus.

  • Increases bone density and muscle tone
  • Sharpens strategic thinking and concentration
  • Exceptional for maintaining joint flexibility

Basketball

A game of verticality, speed, and precision. Basketball keeps you constantly on the move, shifting from offensive sprints to defensive stances in seconds. It is the perfect sport for building explosive power and endurance.

  • Develops explosive jumping power and balance
  • Improves spatial awareness and peripheral vision
  • High caloric burn due to constant motion
